Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

We would happy to provide a list of frequently asked question (FAQ) to delegators  

What is a stake pool?

Stake pool is a group of individuals who pool their resources together to participate in the Cardano proof-of-stake consensus called Ouroboros. By participating in a stake pool, individuals are able to delegate their stake to the pool and assist in the validation of transactions and creation of new blocks on the Cardano blockchain.

A Cardano stake pool run by a Stake Pool Operator (SPO) is a specific stake pool on the Cardano blockchain that is operated and maintained by a single individual or group, known as the SPO. In this case, the stake pool "Free York" is run a Cardano stake pool, who is responsible for setting up and maintaining the necessary infrastructure and software to participate in the Cardano proof-of-stake process.

How are rewards distributed among pool delegators?

The distribution of rewards based on the proportion of ADA that each delegator has staked in the pool. For example, if a delegator has staked 1% of the total ADA in the pool, they will receive 1% of the rewards earned by the pool. Rewards are distributed to delegators after a certain number of epochs, which is usually 4 epochs (20 days) after the pool operator mints a block.

The SPO, as the operator of the pool, will typically take a small percentage of the rewards as a fee for running and maintaining the pool. It's important to note that rewards earned through staking are not fixed, as the reward rate fluctuates based on the total stake in the network, network inflation rate, and the saturation point of the pools.

What is the minimum amount of ADA required to stake?

We recommend delegators have at least 10 ADA to start staking. Actually, the minimum amount of ADA required to stake is 1 ADA. However, there is a one-time registration fee when you first start staking your ADA (2 ADA deposit goes towards registering your stake address on the blockchain and ~0.17 ADA is the transaction fee). The 2 ADA deposit is refundable upon unstaking. It's important to note having a larger amount of ADA staked will result in higher rewards.

How can I check the performance of stake pool?

The performance of a pool can be tracked using various pool-tracking websites such as cexplorer.io or pooltool.io. These websites provide information such as the pool's performance, rewards, and fees. Currently, the performance of Free York Pool can generate a return on investment (ROA) of around ~4.5%.
